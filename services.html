Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with disabilities by providing comprehensive and personalized services that enhance their quality of life, foster independence, and promote inclusivity. We are committed to understanding the unique needs of each individual, offering tailored solutions across various domains, and creating a supportive community that champions the rights and well-being of people with disabilities.

Our Services

Accessibility Consultation

We, “Yes We Care Community Services” specialize in providing comprehensive Accessibility Consultation services designed to enhance the living, working, and recreational environments for individuals with disabilities. Our expert consultants conduct thorough assessments of physical spaces, to identify and eliminate barriers to accessibility.


We work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and challenges, offering tailored recommendations for modifications and assistive technologies that promote independence and inclusivity.

Personal Care Assistance

We are dedicated to providing compassionate and professional Personal Care Assistance services tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals with disabilities. We understand the importance of maintaining independence and dignity, and our highly trained caregivers are committed to offering support in various activities of daily living.


Our Personal Care Assistance services encompass:

  • Personal Hygiene: Assistance with bathing, grooming, and maintaining personal cleanliness to ensure the individual's comfort and well-being.
  • Grooming activities: Helping with dressing and clothing choices, considering individual preferences and ensuring comfort.
  • Meal preparation: Preparing nutritious meals tailored to dietary needs and providing assistance with feeding as required.

    • Accessible Mobility Support Services

    “Yes We Care Community Services” is dedicated to revolutionizing the mobility experience for individuals with disabilities through our specialized Accessible Mobility Services. We prioritize inclusivity, independence, and convenience, offering a range of solutions to meet diverse mobility needs.


    We offer wheelchair-accessible transportation, door-to-door services, and assistance with mobility aids, ensuring seamless and dignified mobility experiences. Our team is dedicated to customizing transportation plans, empowering individuals to maintain independence.

    Financial Assistance and Planning

    Our company is dedicated to empowering individuals with disabilities through comprehensive Financial Assistance and Planning services. We understand the unique financial challenges that can arise, and our team of experts is committed to providing personalized guidance and support to enhance financial well-being.


    We are committed to guiding our clients on a path to financial stability and security. From offering personalized counseling sessions to navigating disability-related benefits and providing assistance with paperwork, we ensure that every aspect of their financial journey is addressed.

    Support Groups

    Our company is dedicated to fostering a sense of community, understanding, and emotional well-being through our Support Group Services for individuals with disabilities. Recognizing the power of shared experiences and mutual support, we provide a platform for individuals facing similar challenges to come together and build meaningful connections.


    Our Support Group Services encompass:

  • Social Activities: Assistance with bathing, grooming, and maintaining personal cleanliness to ensure the individual's comfort and well-being.
  • Leisure Activities: Helping with dressing and clothing choices, considering individual preferences and ensuring comfort.

    • Domestic Services

    Our company is dedicated to ensuring that individuals with disabilities experience comfort, independence, and a well-maintained living environment through our specialized Domestic Services. We understand the unique challenges that may arise in managing daily household activities, and our committed team provides comprehensive support tailored to individual needs.


    Our Domestic Services include:

  • Cleaning and tidying: Offering thorough cleaning services to maintain a hygienic and organized living space, with a focus on creating a comfortable and aesthetically pleasing environment.
  • Grocery Shopping: Facilitating grocery shopping to ensure access to nutritious food.
  • Gardening: Our expert team is dedicated to creating customized gardens, ensuring a therapeutic and inclusive experience for everyone to enjoy.
